MEANING AND STORY BEHIND OUR GROUP NAME

The Story Behind Our Group Name

The group name was chosen by one of our members who is a fan of the series 'Stranger Things'. In the show, the group of four boys demonstrates strong and tough teamwork and loyalty especially during urgent and dangerous situations. Whenever something serious happens, they support one another without hesitation. This inspired us to adopt the same mindset in our research work. Just like in the series, Team Code Red aims to be dependable and united. When one member needs help, the group responds right away. Through this name, we remind ourselves to stay committed, cooperative, and cautious in completing our research successfully.

Meaning of the Group Name

We chose this group name "Code Red" because of its general and common meaning that refers to a situation that requires immediate action, urgency, and also a good teamwork. The word CODE represents programming, logic, problem-solving. While RED symbolizes passion, energy, courage, and leadership. Together, Code Red means a group that codes with passion, thinks with power, and words with unity. For us, it represents alertness and the ability to respond quickly to any kind of challenges that will arise.
